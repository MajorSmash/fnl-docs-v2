Good news.: the simulation is only one component in NinjaLive's water surface generator --- most of the patterns are generated procedurally: exactly to create far backgrounds with no interaction. these video snippets explain:
- https://youtu.be/S5n5Tpd1Gko?list=PLVCUepYV6TvMXN8HQjLU9wKz-G_6JvJsF&t=598
- https://youtu.be/UBNjhb4J8I0?t=461
.
So.: these materials could be used without simulation - or even without ninja (you need to copy only a single file - the basematerial - to your project)
