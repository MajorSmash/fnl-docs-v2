LIVE-2 **SEA SURFACE PATTERNS**
In the video I have referenced ( [Discord](https://discord.com/channels/850913821240983553/1319654945254932510/1499349098443178105) ) I am using a combination of these techniques to build surface patterns:

1. **Simulated patterns**, INSIDE the sim area:
We have a Wave-generator, using the gradient of the landscape beneath the water to generate sine-waves that go towards the coast, the waves are injected into the Navier-Stokes fluid simulation model as velocity --- the rest (waves hitting the coast and the rocks, flowing o surfaces) comes from the surface-aligned fluid simulation.

2. **Passive, non-simulated patterns,** populating the area OUTSIDE the simulation, generated by the Output Material, using two tiled-noise textures, and noises to mix them.
