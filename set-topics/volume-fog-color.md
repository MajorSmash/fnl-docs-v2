Manual, Chapter 24 explains Volumes in general. Briefly: Volume Fog color is defined by the Material Instance, that is applied on the "VolumeCube" mesh. Select the mesh. Go Details. Locate Mat. Open Mat. ---> Check "Albedo" and "Extinction". /// Did you know, that VolumeFog reacts to the color of point-lights placed in the Volume? Try to place coloured lights, as well!
