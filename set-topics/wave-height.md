Wave "height" specifically, is controlled by "MeshDistortion" params. The water surface is a composite of TWO systems:
(A) the interactive simulation, running in a finite area - visualized by the Output Material
(B) non-interactive patterns, entirely generated by the Output Material
This video explains the non-interactive pattern generator: https://youtu.be/S5n5Tpd1Gko?list=PLVCUepYV6TvMXN8HQjLU9wKz-G_6JvJsF&t=598
The MeshDistortion, belonging to the two systems (A and B) is located at two places (two parameter groups) in the Output Material.
A, the sim-driven Mesh.Dist is located under the "MeshDistortion" param group.
B, the non-interactive Mesh.Dist is located under the "TileMap" param group.
