**NINJA as WORLD GENERATOR** (local vs global VFX)
Ninja is originally a local sim solution (character and area FX) - not a world generator: it should be added on top of existing world-scale solutions - eg. snowfields, oceans.
In this video, ninja has been added "on top of" UE native waves: https://youtu.be/-OvCw4Y4480
Here, ninja runs in standalone mode: no world generator, just a plain surface with ninja running: https://youtu.be/ry5ckYEM0uc
This video explains how it is done: https://youtu.be/f2Sqjzf4KKg
.
However: slowly we have started to push ninja in the world-gen way.
This CLOUD field example demonstrates how to generate tiled patterns to populate infinite fields: https://youtu.be/uH2DRdKruaU
The same (as, with clouds) could be done with water - but, we have not made a use-case for this, yet (coming soon! - as a separate use-case package).
