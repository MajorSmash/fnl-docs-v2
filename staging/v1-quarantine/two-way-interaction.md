**TWO WAY INTERACTION** between ninja and agents
By default the cause-effect chain has a preferred direction in the ninja workflow:
(1) objects entering the simulation are being tracked, their position, velocity -and optionally their size - drives simulation density and velocity input.
(2) on the other hand: ninja simulation buffers are exposed to users --- and these buffers (density, velocity, pressure...etc) could be use to build WorldSpace fields - and drive events.
.
In theory we could drive anything (push objects, distort foliage)
In practice, we have been experimenting with niagara - driving particles with ninja generated fields.
